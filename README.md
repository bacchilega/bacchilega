# Hi 👋, I'm Leo Fulvio

A passionate entrepreneur, **CEO and founder of [TestBuddy](https://testbuddy.it)**.  
My mission is to make entrance exam preparation **accessible and affordable**, helping students excel on their tests with powerful quizzes, AI-driven study tools, and insightful analytics—so they can learn more effectively and outperform the competition.

## 🚀 Work
- 🛠 **Currently working on**: Building and scaling TestBuddy as the go-to platform for university entrance exam prep.  
- 🤝 **Open to collaborate on**: Edtech innovations, open-source projects, and data-driven solutions that enhance learning experiences.

## 🎓 About Me
- 💻 I have **4 years of experience** in the digital education sector, blending **data analysis**, **economics**, and **technology** to drive results.  
- 🌱 Constantly exploring ways to leverage automation and AI to improve educational outcomes.  
- 📊 Enthusiastic about turning data into actionable insights for growth and better user experiences.
